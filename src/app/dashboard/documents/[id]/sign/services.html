Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What size businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprise-level organizations. Our solutions are scalable and can be tailored to meet the specific needs and budget of your business.

How long does it take to implement your AI solutions?

Implementation timelines vary depending on the complexity of the solution and your specific requirements. Simple automations can be implemented in a few weeks, while more complex AI systems may take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Do we need technical expertise to use your solutions?

No, our solutions are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to non-technical users. We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively use and benefit from our AI implementations.

How do you measure the success of your services?

We establish clear KPIs and success metrics at the beginning of each project, aligned with your business objectives. We provide regular reports and analytics dashboards that track performance against these metrics, ensuring transparency and measurable results.

What makes Zedzen different from other AI consulting firms?

Our unique combination of AI expertise and deep understanding of delivery marketing sets us apart. We focus on practical, results-driven solutions rather than theoretical applications. Our approach is collaborative, transparent, and centered on driving measurable business value.

How do you handle data security and privacy?

Data security is our top priority. We implement robust security measures and comply with all relevant data protection regulations. All data is encrypted, and we establish clear data handling protocols to ensure your sensitive information remains protected.
