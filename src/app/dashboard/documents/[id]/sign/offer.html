Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How quickly can you start working on my project?

After our initial consultation and agreement on the project scope, we can typically begin work within 1-2 weeks. For urgent projects, we may be able to expedite this timeline.

What information do I need to prepare for the consultation?

It's helpful to have information about your current business challenges, goals, any existing data or systems you're using, and specific areas where you believe AI can help. However, we can guide you through this process if you're unsure.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your AI solutions continue to perform optimally. We can discuss these options during the consultation phase based on your specific needs.

Can you work with our existing technical team?

Absolutely. We often collaborate with in-house teams, providing specialized AI expertise while empowering your team with knowledge transfer and training. This collaborative approach ensures smooth integration and sustainability.
